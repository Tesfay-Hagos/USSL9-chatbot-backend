## Required Documents

### For All International Students

1. **Educational Documents**
   - High school diploma (translated and legalized)
   - University transcripts (if transferring)
   - Diploma Supplement (if available)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - Valid passport (minimum 18 months validity)
   - Passport-size photos (4 copies)

3. **Language Certification**
   - Italian: CELI B2, CILS B2, or equivalent
   - English: B2 or higher (for English-taught programs)

### Additional for Non-EU Students

4. **Declaration of Value** (Dichiarazione di Valore)
   - Issued by the Italian Embassy in your country

5. **Visa Documents**
   - Study visa application
   - Proof of financial means (â‚¬6,000+ per year)
   - Health insurance
   - Accommodation proof

## Admission Requirements by Program

### Bachelor's Programs (Laurea Triennale)
- 12 years of education minimum
- High school diploma with university access qualification
- Italian language B2 (for Italian-taught programs)

### Master's Programs (Laurea Magistrale)
- Bachelor's degree (3+ years)
- Specific prerequisites vary by program
- Higher language requirements (B2-C1)

### PhD Programs (Dottorato)
- Master's degree
- Research proposal
- Interview (in person or online)

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Check Requirements
Visit www.univr.it/en/admissions and verify:
- Your program of interest
- Language requirements
- Specific admission tests

### Step 2: Gather Documents
Collect all required documents. Allow 2-3 months for:
- Translation and legalization
- Declaration of Value (if applicable)

### Step 3: Submit Application

**Non-EU Students:**
Register on www.universitaly.it
- Create account
- Select University of Verona
- Upload documents
- Submit pre-enrollment request

**EU Students:**

### Step 5: Enroll
