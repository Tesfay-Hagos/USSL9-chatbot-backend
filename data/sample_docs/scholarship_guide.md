### How to Apply

1. Obtain your ISEE-U certificate from CAF (Centro Assistenza Fiscale)
2. Register on the ESU Verona portal: https://esu.vr.it
3. Complete the online application
4. Upload required documents
5. Submit before the deadline

### Required Documents

- Valid ID or passport
- ISEE-U certificate
- Academic transcript (for continuing students)
- Residence permit (for non-EU students)
- Bank account details (IBAN)
