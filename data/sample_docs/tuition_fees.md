# University of Verona - Tuition Fees and Payment Guide 2024/2025

## Overview

The University of Verona calculates tuition fees based on family income (ISEE) and the student's course of study. This document provides complete information about fees, deadlines, and payment methods.

## Fee Structure

### Undergraduate Programs (Laurea Triennale)

| ISEE Range | Annual Fee |
|------------|------------|
| Up to €13,000 | €156 (minimum) |
| €13,001 - €20,000 | €300 - €800 |
| €20,001 - €30,000 | €800 - €1,400 |
| €30,001 - €50,000 | €1,400 - €2,200 |
| Over €50,000 | €2,600 (maximum) |

### Graduate Programs (Laurea Magistrale)

Graduate program fees are approximately 15% higher than undergraduate fees for the same ISEE bracket.

### Professional Programs (Medicine, Law, Architecture)

Professional programs may have additional fees of €200-400 per year.

## Payment Deadlines

### First Installment (Prima Rata)
- **Amount**: Minimum €156 + regional tax
- **Deadline**: September 30, 2024
- **Late Payment Fee**: €50 if paid by October 31

### Second Installment (Seconda Rata)
- **Amount**: Calculated based on ISEE
- **Deadline**: March 31, 2025
- **Late Payment Fee**: €100 if paid by April 30

### Third Installment (Terza Rata) - Optional Split
- **Deadline**: May 31, 2025

## How to Pay

### Online Banking (Recommended)
1. Log in to your student portal (ESSE3)
2. Go to "Segreteria" → "Pagamenti"
3. Download the payment slip (MAV/PagoPA)
4. Pay via your bank's online portal

### In Person
- At any bank branch using the MAV slip
- At post offices
- At authorized tobacco shops (for PagoPA)

## Fee Reductions

### ISEE-Based Reductions
Submit your ISEE-U certificate by **November 30, 2024** to receive income-based fee reductions.

### Merit-Based Reductions
- 10% reduction for on-time graduation
- 15% reduction for high GPA (≥28/30)

### Special Categories
- **Disabled students** (≥66%): Full fee exemption
- **Refugees**: Full fee exemption
- **Multiple siblings enrolled**: 20% reduction for second sibling

## Regional Tax (Tassa Regionale)

All students must pay the regional tax of **€176** regardless of ISEE.
